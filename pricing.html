Frequently Asked Questions

What are Excel Coins?

Excel Coins are the virtual currency used on the StudyBuddy platform. You can use them to book sessions with mentors, enroll in courses, and access other premium features. They simplify payments and allow us to offer bonuses and rewards.

Do my coins expire?

No, your purchased Excel Coins never expire. You can use them whenever you like, for as long as your account is active.

Can I get a refund on unused coins?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all coin package purchases. If you're not satisfied, you can request a full refund for any unused coins within 30 days of purchase. Session payments are refunded to your coin balance according to our cancellation policy.

How is payment security handled?

We partner with Instamojo, a leading and secure payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted and processed on their secure servers. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our platform.
